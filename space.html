Here are a few more facts about space!


space is completely silent : Sound waves need a medium to travel through. Since there is no atmosphere in the vacuum of space, the realm between stars will always be eerily silent.


There is an uncountable number of stars in the known universe : An Australian National University study put their estimate at 70 sextillion. Put another way, that's 70,000 million million million.
